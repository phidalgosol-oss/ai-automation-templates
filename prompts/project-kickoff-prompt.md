# Project Kickoff Prompt

Clarifies scope, surfaces blockers, and identifies next steps before you start anything new.

---

## The Prompt

```
I'm about to start a new project. Help me think it through clearly.

Project: [NAME/DESCRIPTION]

Ask me these questions one at a time and wait for my anproject-kickoff-prompt.mdswer before moving on:

1. What does success look like in 30 days? Be specific.
2. Who needs to be involved and what do they need from me?
3. What's the biggest risk or unknown right now?
4. What's the smallest possible version I could ship first?
5. What would make me abandon this? (Important to name it upfront)

After I answer all five, give me:
- A one-sentence project definition I can share with others
- The first 3 actions to take this week
- One assumption I should validate before going further
```

---

## How to use it

Use this before starting anything new — a project, a partnership, a product idea, a content series. Takes 10 minutes and saves hours of misaligned work.

Copy the prompt, replace [NAME/DESCRIPTION] with your project, and paste it into Claude or ChatGPT.
