# Weekly Review Prompt

A structured reflection for professionals who want to close the week with clarity.

---

## The Prompt

```
Run my weekly review. Ask me these questions one at a time and wait for my answer before moving on:

1. What did I actually finish this week? (not what I worked on — what I completed)
2. What didn't get done, and why? Be honest — was it a priority problem or a capacity problem?
3. What drained me this week?
4. What energized me?
5. What's the ONE thing that would make next week feel successful?
6. What do I need to let go of or stop doing?

After I answer all six, give me:
- A 3-sentence summary of my week
- My top 3 priorities for next week
- One thing to watch out for based on what I shared
```

---

## How to use it

Works best on Friday afternoon or Sunday evening. Take 15 minutes. Be honest — no one's reading this but you.

Copy the prompt and paste it into Claude or ChatGPT. Answer each question as it comes. The structure is the point.
